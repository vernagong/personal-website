Smart Scooter Rental

About The Product

A smart scooter rental service. Travelers can choose a scooter to rent and ride near the MRT station.
The smart scooter rental is different from the traditional rental. It can give travelers a sense of stability without having to navigate to find a scooter, because the rental station is near the MRT, and the trouble of traditional rental is also avoided.

This product includes the overall service design of user app to customer service and maintenance app.

Smart Scooter Rental
Makes Travelling More Convenient

For the petty bourgeoisie who takes public transportation to travel, scooter becomes a convenient transportation, but traditional rental often brings some annoyances, such as prior inquiry and evaluation, fear of unknown prices, shortening travel time, etc.
We try to turn the idea that the scooter is only a tool in travel, the scooter can also become part of the travel experience and it is also a solution to reach a balance between scooters and environmental pollution.
In the future, we expect to expand business scopes, such as office workers who are expected to ride scooters but cannot buy and maintain their scooters.

Can I Rent a Scooter Without Worries?

Is it possible to reduce the process and time of scooter rental?

Can I rent a car without restrictions?

Solution

Riders want a quick and easy way to rent a motorcycle. They can upload and verify their driver's license and documents in advance. This allows them to start riding just by scanning the QR code.
We plan a standard process for problem handling. The scooter will automatically be offlined when the battery runs out or the user reports an error via user app, and customer service staff can also create a maintenance order through the customer service system.

When the maintenance order is created, the maintenance app will receive a notification. The manager can assign the order to the maintenance staff. After the maintenance is completed, they can follow the instructions on the App to bring the scooter back online.

Easily Upload Documents

Give clear guidance to help riders upload driver licenses and ID card/passport, and submit them for verification. When the verification has not passed, riders will be blocked to avoid unsafe riding.

Find a Rental Station or Energy Station

Show the location of the rental station and energy station on the map, and navigate to the station. The priority display site on the map will be determined according to whether the user is riding. For example, the map will show the energy stations by default when user is riding.

Quick and Simple to Ride a Scooter

Users can buy the unlimited plans in advance and just choose the purchased plan or time base plan to start riding. During riding, you can get information about locomotive power, accumulated costs, etc., and you can temporarily stop to exchange batteries.

Friendly Payment

Due to the complicated billing and discount methods, we allow the user to pay before the next ride. The checkout page clearly explains the use of the discounts and show the price discounted to help the user understand.

Report Issues

Riding instruction is provided in the app. Users can report issues to us through customer service calls or "Report Issues", making scooter maintenance and monitoring more efficient.
