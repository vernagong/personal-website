Smart Charging
Makes Driving and Charging Management More Amazing

Electric vehicles are becoming more prevalent, and solutions for car charging are even more important. When the car evolves from traditional refueling to charging, waiting for charging and finding a charging station become important; For managers, the charging status, data analysis and business model of the charging station are also a challenge.
Therefore, we try to create overall charging services, such as suggesting charging journeys for drivers, helping managers to manage the charging status, and planning the installation SOP to make charging more convenient.


EV Driver

- User opens Google Maps to find a charging station nearby.
- Drive to charging location and start charging.
- Wait for charging and drive away after fully charged.





User Journey for Drivers

Charging Services

The product is committed to providing a more friendly charging service for EV driving, and helping the owner of charging station to define its business model, and tracking its charging status and cost to maintain a healthy business.


User App / Web

Users can use the App to find charging stations and track the status of charging. If the user do not registered the app, the charging and tracking status can also be used through the webpage.


Web Console

The owners and administrators of the site can instantly view the status of all chargers in the web console, and can easily set prices, view statistical revenue and analyze usage data.


Installer Panel

The installer of the charging station can start the installation according to the guidance of the webpage, and automatically notify the site administrator after the installation is completed.

Principle

Before electric vehicles became widespread, increasing revenue is the motivation for site owners to install charging stations, so it is important for administrators to have easy access to track charging status and data analysis, which would allow them to increase their business without pain.

At the same time, it is necessary to ensure the normal operation of the charging station to make the administrator agree that installing the charging station is a good way to improve customer service and even expand new customers.

Charging Status and Statements

Site owner can track monthly revenue report, statements, revenue analysis, and the detail information of each charging session.

Membership Management

The administrator can send different discount links to customers via SMS. After receiving the link, customer can install the membership discount into their EV app and get free/discount charging sessions.

Pricing

The pricing page allows site owners adjust their pricing rules due to different situations such as peak time or off-peak time. The price rule will be presented in the user app to inform users.

Demand Response

Utility companies are responding to growing demand for electricity with Demand Response programs to help alleviate high electricity usage rates. Managers will receive text messages on the phone informing them of upcoming DR events and view reports with detailed electricity usage statistics.

Installation Process

This is a tool that guide the electricians installing a EV charger step by step. It also helps owner tracking the whole installation process on the web page.

Multi-level Management

This was designed to be customized with different levels of accounts for different types of customers, such as co-branding partners, headquarters, and site owners.
