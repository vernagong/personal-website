BetGame

Apr. 2019
BetGame is a platform that opens voting and purchases virtual currency for current events. The value of virtual currency increases or decreases with the turnout and results.

Popular and Classified Current Events

Common current affairs voting is only for politics. We have expanded the scope to finance, sports, entertainment, etc., and categorized them and prioritized popular events.

Chat with Like-minded People

Discuss insights on current events or virtual coins in a chat room.

The Rise and Fall of Virtual Currency

The more voting options will affect the rise or fall of the virtual currency. Users can decide voting options to invest based on the rise and fall, and can view the success rate of past votes.
